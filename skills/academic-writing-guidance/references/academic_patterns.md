# Academic patterns

## What this skill is
A guidance layer for scholarly writing that emphasizes thesis clarity, evidence discipline, and explicit argument structure.

## How to use it
Activate it for requests like:
- "turn these notes into a literature review"
- "strengthen this thesis section"
- "draft an academic essay outline"
- "rewrite this in a scholarly tone"

## Default structure

### Thesis / purpose
One concise statement of the main argument or research aim.

### Main body
- core claim
- supporting reasoning or evidence
- counterargument or limitation where needed

### Conclusion
Restate the contribution or implication without repeating the full draft.

## Writing checklist
- Is the central claim explicit?
- Are key terms defined when needed?
- Are evidence and interpretation distinguished?
- Are unsupported assertions removed or labeled?
- Is the conclusion consistent with the argument?
